Paella!

Description:

Paella is a rice dish originally from Valencia.
While non-Spaniards commonly view it as Spain's national dish,
Spaniards almost unanimously consider it to be a dish from the Valencian region.
Valencians, in turn, regard paella as one of their identifying symbols.
It is one of the best-known dishes in Spanish cuisine.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Saffron
  • Vegetables
  • Chicken
  • Seafood
  • Lots of love.

Steps:

  1. Flavour the pan
  2. Gently fry the rice
  3. Build a rich sauce
  4. Prepare the saffron
  5. Boil for 5 minutes
  6. Add meat, and seafood and cook for 12 minutes
  7. Rest for 5 minutes, and serve!
